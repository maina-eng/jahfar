Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta or Bacon
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs with grated cheese.
  4. Mix hot pasta with egg-cheese mixture and pancetta.
  5. Serve with extra cheese and pepper.

Nutritional Info (approx.)

Calories: 500 | Protein: 20g | Fat: 18g | Carbs: 60g

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • Long-grain basmati rice
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger & Garlic paste
  • Spices: Garam masala, cumin, coriander, cardamom
  • Mint leaves
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Fried onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for at least an hour.
  2. Fry sliced onions in oil until golden brown, then set aside.
  3. Boil the rice until 70% cooked (parboiled), drain water.
  4. In a large pot, layer the chicken, rice, fried onions, and mint/coriander leaves.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fried onions and fresh herbs, and serve with raita or salad.

Nutritional Info (approx.)

Calories: 700 | Protein: 30g | Fat: 35g | Carbs: 70g
